# Navbar Creative Sketches

Goal: explore ultra-premium SaaS navbar directions before committing to production changes.

## Variants

1. `variant-a-obsidian-pill.html`
   - Calm frosted dock, high-contrast Conference pill, compact enterprise spacing.
   - Best for: clear hierarchy and conversion-first UX.

2. `variant-b-editorial-glassline.html`
   - Thinner glassline navbar with refined typography and subtle active rail.
   - Best for: modern editorial-tech brand presence.

3. `variant-c-product-led-signal.html`
   - Product-style nav with stronger informational hierarchy and secondary meta badge.
   - Best for: SaaS positioning with enterprise confidence.

## Evaluation Criteria

- Premium visual quality without flashy effects
- Readability and hierarchy at a glance
- Conference emphasis elegance (noticeable, not loud)
- CTA balance and non-collapsing geometry
- Feasible translation to existing React + Tailwind implementation

## Recommendation

Start from **Variant A** as baseline and borrow typography finesse from **Variant B**.
